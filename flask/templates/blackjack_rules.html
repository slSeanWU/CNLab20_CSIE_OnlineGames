Rules

Try to beat dealer without going over 21.
Dealer must stand on all 17s.
The value of cards 2 through 10 is their pip value.
Face cards are all worth 10.
Aces can be worth 1 or 11.

Pay Table

Blackjack pays 3 to 2.
